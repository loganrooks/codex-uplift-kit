# research-audit compaction prompt

Compact this research/audit session.

Preserve source-grounded findings, citations or file paths, claim status, open questions, artifact paths, reviewed sources, rejected/unsupported claims, and verification gaps.

Keep observations, inferences, recommendations, and stakeholder decisions distinct. If a source did not address a topic, preserve that limitation. Do not turn adjacent paraphrase into false attribution. Do not preserve raw source excerpts beyond what is needed to identify the claim and source.

Structure the output as: audit objective; sources checked; source-grounded findings; inferred findings; decisions/open questions; artifacts; verification gaps; next audit steps.
